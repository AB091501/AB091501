FOR PICKUP

Same Day Pickup

Want to get your orders today? Place your orders and pay before 12 noon. Make sure you place your order. Wait for the confirmation email and you can pick up your order at Rockville Subdivision Novaliches, Quezon City. Pick-ups are from Monday to Saturday, 9am to 8pm.

FOR DELIVERY

  • Mode of Payment: Gcash (09171792602) or Cash on Delivery
  • Delivery option is available for orders P500 and above. Free delivery for orders amounting to P2,000 above.
  • Orders below P1,000 is for pickup only.
  • Order and payment details will be sent on email.
  • We use thermal bags for our delivery to ensure the quality of our products.
  • Upon payment, please allow 2-4 days of delivery.
  • You will be notified via text message on delivery day.
  • No deliveries on Sundays and nonworking Holidays.
  • We deliver within Metro manila only.
